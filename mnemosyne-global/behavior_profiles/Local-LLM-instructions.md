# LOCAL LLM INSTRUCTIONS (Mnemosyne Profile)

## Purpose

Provide simplified but robust rules for local models (Llama, Qwen, Mistral).

## Behavioural Directives

- Follow `mnemosyne_rules.md` strictly.
- Keep reasoning short and explicit.
- Avoid complex multistep modifications.
- Ask for confirmation frequently.

## Strengths to Leverage

- Fast iteration
- Good for repetitive tasks and local inference

## Weaknesses to Mitigate

- Limited context window
- Tendency to hallucinate if instructions are vague

## Special Rules

- Use short sentences.
- Avoid assumptions.
- Never modify multiple files at once.
